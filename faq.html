Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main ingredient for chocolate?

The main ingredient for chocolate is cocoa beans, which are roasted and processed to produce cocoa mass, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. These components are then combined with sugar, milk (for milk chocolate), and other ingredients to create the final chocolate product.

What types of chocolates do you offer?

We offer a wide range of chocolates including dark chocolate, milk chocolate, white chocolate, and a variety of unique flavors and fillings. Our selection is crafted to cater to every chocolate lover's taste.

Are your chocolates handmade?

Yes, all of our chocolates are carefully handcrafted by our skilled chocolatiers. We take pride in our artisanal approach to ensure the highest quality in every bite.

Do you use high-quality ingredients?

Absolutely! We source the finest ingredients, including premium Belgian chocolate, to create our delicious confections. Quality is our top priority.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a selection of vegan and gluten-free chocolates. Please check our product descriptions or contact us for more information on specific items.

How should I store my chocolates?

To maintain the best quality, store your chocolates in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and strong odors. The ideal temperature is between 60-70°F (15-21°C).
